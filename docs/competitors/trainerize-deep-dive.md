# Trainerize (ABC Trainerize) - Deep Competitor Analysis

## Summary

Trainerize is the **global market leader** in online coaching platforms. While not a direct threat in the Egyptian market (no Arabic support, USD pricing), it's the product benchmark we're measured against by coaches who've heard of it. Understanding Trainerize's strengths — and the gaps that make it unsuitable for Egypt — sharpens our positioning.

## Pricing (2026 — Confirmed Public Tiers)

Trainerize uses a **base plan + paid add-ons** model. The headline price is low, but real costs escalate quickly.

### Base Plans

| Plan | Monthly Price | Client Limit | Key Inclusions |
|------|:------------:|:------------:|----------------|
| **Basic (Free)** | $0 | 1 client | Workout builder, exercise library, basic messaging |
| **Grow** | $9/mo | 2 clients | + AI Workout Builder, Trainerize.me storefront, wearable integrations, Zapier |
| **Pro 5** | $23/mo | 5 clients | + Custom branded app option |
| **Pro 50** | $120/mo | 50 clients | Scales with client count |
| **Pro 100** | $79–120/mo | 100 clients | Varies by plan structure |
| **Pro 200** | $225–275/mo | 200 clients | Highest individual tier |
| **Studio Plus** | $248/mo per location | 500–1,000 clients | All add-ons included |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Custom | Multi-location, custom onboarding |

### Paid Add-Ons (on Grow + Pro plans)

| Add-On | Cost | What It Unlocks |
|--------|:----:|-----------------|
| Advanced Nutrition (Smart Meal Planner) | $20–45/mo | Full meal planning, 490+ recipe library, macro tracking |
| Stripe Integrated Payments | $10/mo | Accept payments in-app |
| Business Tools | $25/mo | Scheduling, automated follow-ups |
| Video Coaching | $10/mo | Video calls with clients |
| Custom Branded App | $169 one-time + $99/yr Apple fee | Coach's brand in App Store |

### Real-World Cost for a Solo Coach (50 clients)

| Component | Monthly Cost |
|-----------|:----------:|
| Pro 50 base | $120 |
| Smart Meal Planner | $45 |
| Stripe Payments | $10 |
| Business Tools | $25 |
| **Total** | **$200/mo (~10,000 EGP)** |

The add-on model means **headline prices are misleading**. A coach who needs nutrition + payments + business tools pays 3–5x the base price.

## What to Learn From Trainerize

### 1. The Add-On Model Is a Trap

Coaches hate discovering that the $23/mo plan actually costs $120/mo with the features they need. **Lesson:** Our all-inclusive tiered pricing is a genuine differentiator. Never gate basic features behind add-ons.

### 2. Automated Program Delivery Is Great UX

Set a program to auto-deliver on a schedule (e.g., new program every 4 weeks). This saves coach time. Worth building in V2.

### 3. The Storefront Concept Works

Trainerize.me lets coaches sell programs publicly. Our "coach profile page" and "template marketplace" in V3 serve the same purpose — validated by Trainerize's success with it.

### 4. Group Programs Fill a Gap

While Dragon doesn't use groups, many coaches run challenges and bootcamps. Keep this on the roadmap for V3.

### 5. Wearable Integration Is Expected

Coaches globally expect Apple Watch, Garmin, Fitbit sync. Plan for this in Year 2 API integrations.

## Bottom Line

Trainerize is the global gold standard, but it's **priced out of Egypt** and **completely unlocalized** for the Arab world. No Arabic, no Egyptian payments, no offline support, and a deceptive add-on pricing model that turns a $23 plan into a $200 bill.

We don't need to beat Trainerize globally. We need to be the **Trainerize of Egypt** — same caliber features, built for Arabic-speaking coaches, priced in EGP, and working from a phone in a gym basement with no WiFi.
