# FitXpert - Deep Competitor Analysis

## Summary

FitXpert is our **#1 direct competitor** in the Egyptian market. They are the closest thing to what we're building — a B2B2C SaaS platform for fitness coaches and nutrition centers in Egypt/MENA, with Arabic support, client management, workout programming, diet plans, and progress tracking.

They are ahead of us: founded in 2023, funded ($1.3M seed in Jan 2026), live on both app stores, and actively expanding to Gulf markets. This is the company we must study, learn from, and then surpass.

### ElCoach Connection?

No confirmed direct connection between FitXpert and ElCoach. They are separate companies:
- **ElCoach** = B2C app (sells directly to end-users, AI-generated workouts). Founded by different team.
- **FitXpert** = B2B2C platform (sells to coaches, coaches serve clients). Founded by Salah Selim & Mostafa Mahmoud.

However, they operate in the same ecosystem (Egyptian fitness tech) and likely know each other. FitXpert solves the problem ElCoach doesn't: coach-to-client management. ElCoach replaces the coach; FitXpert empowers the coach. Same market, opposite philosophies — and FitXpert's model is identical to ours.

## Pricing (What We Know)

FitXpert does **not** publicly list pricing. Their pricing page returns a 500 error as of June 2026. From available intel:

| Signal | What It Suggests |
|--------|-----------------|
| "Subscription tiers by client count" | Similar to Trainerize model — tiered SaaS |
| "Individual trainers + larger corporate deployments" | They have both solo coach and enterprise pricing |
| No public pricing page | Likely sales-led for larger accounts, possibly self-serve for small coaches |
| Funded $1.3M — needs revenue | Prices likely higher than what free/WhatsApp coaches expect |

### Estimated Pricing (Based on Market Positioning)

Given they serve clinics + centers + individual trainers, likely:
- Solo trainer: 200-500 EGP/mo (or per-client pricing)
- Nutrition center/clinic: 1,000-5,000 EGP/mo (based on team size)
- Enterprise/gym chain: Custom pricing

Their lack of public pricing is a **weakness we can exploit** — transparent pricing builds trust with independent coaches.

## What to Learn From FitXpert

### 1. Their Positioning Was Smart

They positioned as "infrastructure" early — this likely helped them raise funding because investors understand SaaS infrastructure businesses. We should learn: even if we market to coaches simply, we should position to investors as "operating system for fitness professionals."

### 2. They Expanded Beyond Just Coaches

Serving nutrition centers and clinics expanded their TAM (Total Addressable Market). We should plan for this in Year 2 — nutritionists and physiotherapy clinics are adjacent markets.

### 3. They Got to Gulf Markets Early

By having London HQ and Gulf investor, they've pre-positioned for Saudi/UAE expansion. We should note: when we expand, we need Gulf connections too.

### 4. They Didn't Crack Growth

5K installs after 3 years of operation (2023-2026) is... not impressive. Their product might be good but their growth engine is weak. This validates our Dragon-as-content-engine strategy. FitXpert likely didn't have a "coach champion" creating content for them.

### 5. Their Feature Set Validates Our Roadmap

Almost everything we planned (workouts, diet, check-ins, progress tracking, chat, Arabic) — they already built. This is good news: it proves the market wants these features. Our job is to do them BETTER, not different.

## Bottom Line

FitXpert is real, funded, and building what we're building. But they have NOT won the market. 5K installs after 3 years means they haven't found product-market fit in terms of growth — likely because they're selling top-down to clinics/businesses rather than bottom-up to individual coaches.

**Our edge:** We start with one coach (Dragon), nail HIS workflow, and grow through word-of-mouth + content. We're mobile-first, offline-first, coach-obsessed, and transparent. FitXpert is enterprise-first, web-first, and opaque.

The race is not over. They have funding; we have focus.
